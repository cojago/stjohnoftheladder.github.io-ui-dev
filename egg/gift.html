Theosis

Theosis, or deification, is the transformative process by which a believer becomes more like God through His grace. It involves a deepening relationship with God, characterized by spiritual growth, participation in the sacraments, prayer, and living a life of virtue. Theosis signifies the ultimate purpose of human life: to partake in the divine nature and reflect God's holiness and love. It is a lifelong journey of becoming united with God, growing in faith and holiness.
